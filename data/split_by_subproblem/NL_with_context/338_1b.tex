\( \bar{T} = \int_{s_a}^{s_e} T \, dS = h \)  

\( \bar{T} = \frac{\int_{s_a}^{s_e} T \, dS}{s_a - s_e} = \frac{h_a - h_e}{s_a - s_e} \)
