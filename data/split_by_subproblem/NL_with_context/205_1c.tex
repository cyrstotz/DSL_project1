\( \dot{S}_{erz} = \dot{Q}_{out} \cdot (1/T_{KF} - 1/T_{Reactor}) \)

\( \dot{S}_{erz} = \dot{Q}_{out} \cdot (1/T_{KF} - 1/373.15) \)
