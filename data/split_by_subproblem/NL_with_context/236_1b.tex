Steady-state flow process with \( \dot{m} \):  
\( 0 = \dot{m} [s_e - s_a] + \dot{Q} / T + \dot{S}_{erz} \)  

Simplified:  
\( \dot{Q} = \dot{m} [s_a - s_e] \)  
\( T = \dot{Q} / \dot{m} [s_a - s_e] \)  

Since the cooling jacket is adiabatic:  
\( \dot{Q} = \dot{Q}_{aus} \)  

Calculation:  
\( T = 62.182 \, \text{kJ/s} / \dot{m} [s_a - s_e] \)  
\( T = 62.182 \, \text{kJ/s} / \dot{m} [c] \)
