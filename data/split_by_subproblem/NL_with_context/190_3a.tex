\( pV = mRT \)

\( m_g = \frac{p_{g,1} \cdot V_{g,1}}{R \cdot T_{g,1}} = \frac{1.1 \, \text{bar} \cdot 3.14 \, \text{L}}{0.1663 \, \text{kJ/kg·K} \cdot 500^\circ \text{C}} = 2.686 \, \text{g} \)
