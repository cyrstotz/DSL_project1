- Reference temperature \( T_R = 100^\circ \) Celsius,
- Reference heat flow rate \( \dot{Q}_R = 100 \) kilowatts,

The heat output rate \( \dot{Q}_{\text{aus}} \) is calculated as:
\[ \dot{Q}_{\text{aus}} = \dot{Q}_R \frac{T_{\text{ein}}}{T_{\text{aus}}}, \]
