The mass flow rate \( \dot{m}_{R134a} \) is calculated using the energy balance:  
\( 0 = \dot{m} (h_e - h_a) + \dot{Q} - \dot{W} \).  
Rearranging gives:  
\( \frac{\dot{W}_{K}}{h_e - h_a} = \dot{m} \),  
which simplifies to:  
\( \dot{m} = \frac{\dot{W}_{K}}{h_2 - h_3} \).
