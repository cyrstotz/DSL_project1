\( T_{KF} = \frac{\int T dS}{s_a - s_e} \)  

From the table: Differential \( dS \) is isobaric.  

\( \int T dS = \Delta U = c_{if} (T_e - T_a) \)  

\( s_a - s_e = c_{if} \ln \left( \frac{T_e}{T_a} \right) \)  

\( T_{KF} = \frac{T_e - T_a}{\ln \left( \frac{T_e}{T_a} \right)} = 293.122 \, \text{K} \)
