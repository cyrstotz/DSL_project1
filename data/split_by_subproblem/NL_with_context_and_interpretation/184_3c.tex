\( \Delta E = m \cdot (u_2 - u_1) = \int Q \)  
\( \Rightarrow u_2 - u_1 = c_V \cdot (T_2 - T_1) \)  
\( = 0.633 \cdot 10^{-3} \cdot (500 - 0) \)
