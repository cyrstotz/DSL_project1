\[ \frac{\text{d}E}{\text{d}t} = \sum \dot{m} (h + \text{ke} + \text{pe}) + \sum \dot{Q} - \sum \dot{W} \]  

This is simplified to:  
\[ 0 = m (h_e - h_a + \frac{w_e^2}{2} - \frac{w_a^2}{2}) \]
