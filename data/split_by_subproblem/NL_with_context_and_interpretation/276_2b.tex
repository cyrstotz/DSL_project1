\( T_6 = T_5 \cdot \left( \frac{p_6}{p_5} \right)^{\frac{k-1}{k}} \),

\( T_6 = 451.9 \, \text{K} \cdot \left( \frac{0.191}{0.5} \right)^{\frac{1.4-1}{1.4}} \).
