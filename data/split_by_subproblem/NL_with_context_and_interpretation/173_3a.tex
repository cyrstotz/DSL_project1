**Ideal gas law:**  
\( p \cdot V = m \cdot R \cdot T \)

\( T_1 = 500^\circ \text{C} = 773.15 \, \text{K} \), \( p_1 = 140094 \, \text{Pa} \), \( V_1 = 3.141 \, \text{L} = 0.003141 \, \text{m}^3 \)

\( m_g = \frac{p_1 \cdot V_1}{R \cdot T_1} \)
