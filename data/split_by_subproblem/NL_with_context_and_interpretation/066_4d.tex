Find \( E_k = \frac{Q_{zu}}{\dot{W}} \) with \( \dot{W} = 28 \, \text{W} \).  

Energy balance from state 1 to state 5:  
\( \dot{m} (h_1 - h_2) + \dot{Q} = 0 \).  

\( \dot{Q} = \dot{m} (h_2 - h_1) \).  

Substitute:  
\( \dot{Q} = 4 \, \text{kg/h} \cdot \frac{3600}{h} \cdot (234.22 - 93.49) \).  

\( \dot{Q} = 9.173 \, \text{kW} \).  

\( E_k = \frac{Q_{zu}}{\dot{W}} = \frac{9.173}{28} \).  

\( E_k = 6.18 \).
