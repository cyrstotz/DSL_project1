\[ T_2 = T_h \cdot \left( \frac{p_2}{p_1} \right)^{(n-1)/n} \]  

Rearranging:  
\[ p_2 = p_1 \cdot \left( \frac{T_2}{T_h} \right)^{n/(n-1)} \]  

Substituting values:  
\[ c_v = 0.633 \, \text{kJ/kg·K}, \, R = \frac{8.314}{50} = 0.166 \, \text{kJ/kg·K}, \, c_p = c_v + R = 0.789 \, \text{kJ/kg·K} \]  
\[ \frac{c_p}{c_v} = n = 1.263 \]
