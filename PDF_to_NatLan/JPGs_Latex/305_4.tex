The piston pressure is determined using the formula:  
\[

\]

\[
p_{g,1} = 100 \, \text{kPa} + 35.965 \, \text{kPa} = 135.97 \, \text{kPa}
\]

\[
m_g = \frac{V_{g,1} \cdot p_{g,1}}{R \cdot T_{g,1}}

\[
