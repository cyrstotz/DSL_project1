\[
m_{2u2} - m_{1u1} = \Delta m h + \Sigma Q

\[
m_{2u2} - m_{1u1} - Q = \Delta m h
\]  
Thus:  
\[
\Delta m = \frac{m_{2u2} - m_{1u1} - Q}{h}
\]

\[
