The volumetric exergy destruction rate is given by:  
\[
\dot{E}_{x,\text{vol.}} = T_0 \cdot \dot{S}_{\text{erz}}
\]  
This simplifies to:  
\[
e_{x,\text{vol.}} = T_0 \cdot S_{\text{erz}}
\]
