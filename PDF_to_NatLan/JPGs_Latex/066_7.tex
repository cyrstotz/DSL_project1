\[
\dot{m} (h_5 - h_1) + \dot{Q} = 0

Rearranging:  
\[
\dot{Q} = \dot{m} (h_1 - h_5)
\]

\[
\dot{Q} = 4 \, \text{kg/h} \cdot \frac{1}{3600} \cdot (234.22 - 93.49)

\[
\dot{Q} = 9.173 \, \text{kW}
\]  

Coefficient of performance:  
\[
\epsilon_K = \frac{\dot{Q}_{\text{zu}}}{\dot{W}_K} = \frac{9.173}{28} = 6.18
