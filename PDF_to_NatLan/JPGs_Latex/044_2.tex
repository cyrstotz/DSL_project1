\[
\Delta ex_{\text{flow}} = u_6 - u_0 - T_0(s_6 - s_0) + p_0(v_6 - v_0)

\[
u_6 - u_0 = c_p(T_6 - T_0) - T_0 \ln \left(\frac{T_6}{T_0}\right)

\[

\]  
Numerical evaluation yields:  
\[
\Delta ex_{\text{flow}} = -11.0723 \, \text{kJ/kg}
