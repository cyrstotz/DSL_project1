\[
\epsilon_K = \frac{\dot{Q}_K}{\dot{W}_K}
\]  

From the energy balance at the evaporator:  
\[

\[
\dot{Q}_K = \dot{m} \, [h_2 - h_a]

h_2 = h_f = 21.12 \, \frac{\text{kJ}}{\text{kg}}

For \( \epsilon_K \):
