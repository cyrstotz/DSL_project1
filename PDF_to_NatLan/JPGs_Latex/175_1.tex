\[
s_{\text{vap,in}} = s_f + x \cdot (s_g - s_f)

\[

\[
s_{\text{vap,out}} = s_f + x \cdot (s_g - s_f)
\]

\[
x = 0.8143
\]  
\[
s_{\text{vap,out}} = 1.3069 + 0.814 \cdot (7.3549 - 1.3069)
\]  
\[
s_{\text{vap,out}} = 6.2298 \, \frac{\text{kJ}}{\text{kg·K}}
\]  

The entropy production rate is given by:  
\[

\[
