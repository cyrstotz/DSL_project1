The energy balance equation is written as:  
\[
\frac{dE}{dt} = \dot{Q} + \dot{m} \cdot h_2 - \dot{m} \cdot h_1
\]  
This simplifies to:  
\[
\Delta Q = m_g \cdot c_V \cdot (T_1 - T_2)
\]  
Substituting values:  
\[
\Delta Q = 0.633 \cdot (500 - 0) \cdot 0.005
\]  
\[
\Delta Q = 316.5 \, \text{J}
\]
