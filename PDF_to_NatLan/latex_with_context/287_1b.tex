Thermodynamic mean temperature \( T_{\text{KF}} \):  
\[
T_{\text{KF}} = \frac{\int_{T_1}^{T_2} c_p \, dT}{\Delta s}
\]

\[
s_2 - s_1 = \int_{T_1}^{T_2} \frac{c_p}{T} \, dT = c_p \ln \left( \frac{T_2}{T_1} \right)
\]
