The thermodynamic mean temperature \( \bar{T} \) is calculated as:  
\[
\bar{T} = \frac{\Delta h}{\Delta S}, \, \text{the ideal fluid wedge}
\]  
Using the formula:  
\[
\bar{T} = \frac{T_2 - T_1}{\ln\left(\frac{T_2}{T_1}\right)} = 203.12 \, \text{K}
\]
