The pressure \( p_{g,1} \) is calculated using the ideal gas law:  
\[
p_{g,1} = \frac{m_g R T}{V}
\]

\[

Rearranging:  
\[
p_{g,1} = \frac{m_{\text{EW}} \cdot g}{A} + \frac{m_K \cdot g}{A} + p_{\text{atm}}
\]

\[
