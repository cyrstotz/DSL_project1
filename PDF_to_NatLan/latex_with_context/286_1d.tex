\[
\Delta E = \Delta U = m (\mu_1 - \mu_2)

\[
\mu_1 = x_D \cdot u_{\text{liq}}(70^\circ\text{C}) + (1 - x_D) \cdot u_{\text{liq}}(100^\circ\text{C})
\]  
Where \( x_D = 0.005 \).  

The specific internal energy at state 2 is:  
\[
\mu_2 = u_{\text{liq}}(70^\circ\text{C})
\]

\[
