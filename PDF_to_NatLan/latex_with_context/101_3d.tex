\[
p_{\text{K}} = p_{\text{amb}} + \frac{F}{A}
\]

\[

\[

\]  
\[
p_{\text{K}} = 1 \cdot 10^5 + \frac{313.92}{7.854 \cdot 10^{-3}} = 1.3983944 \cdot 10^5 \, \text{Pa}

\]
