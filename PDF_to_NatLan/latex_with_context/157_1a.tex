\[
0 = \dot{m} \, (h_{\text{in}} - h_{\text{out}}) + \dot{Q}_R + \dot{Q}_{\text{out}} - \dot{W}.
\]  

Rearranging to solve for the heat flow removed by the coolant:  
\[
\dot{Q}_{\text{out}} = -\dot{Q}_R + \dot{m} \, (h_{\text{out}} - h_{\text{in}}).
\]
