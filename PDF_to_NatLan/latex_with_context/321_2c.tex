\[

\[
\Delta ex_{\text{flow}} = 85.4597 \, \frac{\text{kJ}}{\text{kg}} - 73.2946 \, \frac{\text{kJ}}{\text{kg}} + 108.621 \, \frac{\text{kJ}}{\text{kg}} = 120.786 \, \frac{\text{kJ}}{\text{kg}}
