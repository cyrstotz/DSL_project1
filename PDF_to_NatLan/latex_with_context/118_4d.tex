The coefficient of performance \( \epsilon_K \) is calculated as:  
\[
\epsilon_K = \frac{\dot{Q}_{\text{zu}}}{\dot{W}_{\text{K}}}
\]  

Substituting:  
\[
\epsilon_K = \frac{\dot{Q}_{\text{zu}}}{\dot{Q}_{\text{obl}} - \dot{Q}_{\text{aul}}}
\]  

Where:  
\[
\dot{Q}_{\text{zu}} = \dot{Q}_{\text{K}}
\]
