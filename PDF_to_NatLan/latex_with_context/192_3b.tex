To determine \( p_{g,2} \) and \( T_{g,2} \):  

The equilibrium condition states:  
\[
p_{g,2} = p_{g,1}
\]  

Thus:  
\[
p_{g,2} = 1.4 \, \text{bar}
\]  

Since \( \dot{Q} = 0 \), the gas temperature \( T_{g,2} \) must equal the EW temperature \( T_{\text{EW,2}} \).  

From Table 1:  
\[
T_{\text{sat}}(p = 1.4 \, \text{bar}) = 0.000^\circ\text{C} = T_{g,2}
\]
